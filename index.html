My university projects:

Paradox Paradise

For the "Game Design I" subject, I designed a tabletop party game. In this project I was in charge of all of the design of the game, as well as the visuals, and documentation. The video tutorial for the game can be seen here.

M.A.D. Barcelona

For the "Game Design II" subject. I was in charge of the narrative design, including the worldbuilding, the characters, and most of the dialogues. I was also part of designing combat mechanics, although these aren't present in the text adventure and are only found in the GDD. Finally, I was the main person working on the documentation.

GotG: Battle for the Universe

Game for the "Project III" subject. I was in charge of the narrative design, adapting the mechanics to the lore of the Marvel Unvierse. I was also the main designer working on the player, iterating existing designs, and coming up with new abilities. Everything I've done in this project can be seen here.

Who Toasted Mr Toaster?

This is a VR Game designed for the "AR/VR" subject. For this project I was one of the main designers behind the conceptualization. I designed all puzzles and storybeats, as well as the characters, the layout of the map, and the interactions with the environment. A playthrough of the game can be seen here.

Bacon Backlog

This was an app concept designed for the "Gamification" subject. For this project I came up with the concept, and I designed most of the app's mechanics and theme.
